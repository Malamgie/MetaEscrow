Frequently Asked Questions

Once you submit your proof of payment via the Wallet tab, our administrative team will verify the transfer against our bank records. This process typically takes between 15 minutes to 2 hours during normal business hours.
If either party raises a dispute, the funds locked in escrow are permanently frozen. Both parties will be asked to submit evidence (receipts, pictures, chat logs). A MetaEscrow administrator will review the evidence and issue a final, binding resolution, either refunding the buyer or paying the seller.
Know Your Customer (KYC) verification is required to maintain a secure and fraud-free environment. Unverified accounts have a strict daily withdrawal limit of ₦500,000. Verified accounts enjoy increased limits, a "Verified" badge to build trust, and priority support.
MetaEscrow charges a standard 1% fee on all completed escrow transactions. This fee is automatically deducted from the seller's payout upon successful completion of the transaction. Wallet deposits and withdrawals are entirely free.
