How MetaEscrow Works

A simple, five-step process that guarantees safety for all parties.

1. Agree

Buyer and seller agree to terms and create a transaction.

2. Fund

Buyer transfers the funds securely into the MetaEscrow vault.

3. Deliver

Seller is notified funds are secured and ships the product/service.

4. Inspect

Buyer receives the item and approves the delivery on the platform.

5. Release

MetaEscrow automatically releases the funds to the seller's wallet.
